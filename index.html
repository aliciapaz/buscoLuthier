Luthier search works in four simple steps.

1. Search:

It's simple. On luthier search homepage, enter the name of a luthier you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search icon on the top menu to view the list of all luthiers available.

2. Locate:

Get to see where the luthier's workshop is located by clicking on the luthier profile. You can get the directions from anywhere in the city.

3. Contact:

Send a message to the luthier to schedule an appointment.

4. Enjoy:

Once your instrument is fixed, you can now enjoy it! Check out our blog to see what we recommend when looking for a luthier, how to take care of your musical instruments and other cool stuff.
